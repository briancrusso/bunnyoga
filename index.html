Yoga Bunny

I've been drawing since I can remember.
I grew up in Short Hills, NJ, then moved to New York and earned a degree in Film from NYU.
Afterwards, I discovered something I love even as much as drawing and making films...
doing Yoga.

I earned a teaching certificate from Yoga to the People in 2010, during which time I developed the
Yoga Bunny illustrations.

Since then, I've receieved hundreds of emails from people all over the world, praising these illustrations.
I'm thrilled that my artwork has reached so many people, and am excited to announce that a Yoga Bunny book is in development with Harper Collins Publishing.
