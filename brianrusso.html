Introduction

I am an author, illustrator, and web coder. I believe creating simple and elegant websites and apps is the best way to share my ideas.

I believe in finishing things that I start, excelling through trial and error, and having fun with things I work on.

I also believe that going through an entire jar of peanut butter in one sitting is sometimes okay.

Where are you from?

I was born in Glen Ellyn, Illinios. When I was six I moved to Short Hills, New Jersey, where I grew up. After high school, I moved to New York City, and have lived here ever since.

More About Me

What are your hobbies?

I enjoy moving around a lot and making people laugh. Over the past couple years that has translated in yoga, dancing, and improv/stand-up comedy.

What would be your dream job?

I’d love to work with a small creative team, constantly coming up with funny stories, characters, and scenarios. Using my illustration, writing, and web-coding skills in tandem would be a really exciting challenge. I’d also like to work in an environment that is project-based and deadline driven. So, some examples would be:

  • Writing for a tv or web Comedy Series
  • Storyboarding for a show like Adventure Time
  • Writing and Illustrating Children's Books
  • creating funny, interative content for children online
